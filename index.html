Hi, I'm Saeed.

I am a Postdoctoral Fellow at the Steve & Cindy Rasmussen Institute for Genomic Medicine, Nationwide Children's Hospital, Columbus, OH.

My current research interests are Computational Immunology, NK cell modeling, and Stochastic Processes.

For my PhD, I worked on first-passage problems in stochastic systems with resetting. I also have experience in theoretical biophysics, virus capsid assembly, and multiscale in silico modeling of CAR-NK cell therapies.
