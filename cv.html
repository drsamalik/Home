Research Interests

Computational Immunology & NK cell modeling

Multiscale in silico models of Natural Killer (NK) cell responses. Predicting CAR-NK cytotoxicity across target cell types; clonal variability in early NK responses; spatial statistics of resting-state receptor organization; Protein Language Models.

Stochastic processes and first-passage problems

First-passage theory under stochastic resetting. Effects of dimension, attractive bias, potential barriers, and time overheads on optimal resetting-rate vanishing transitions.

Theoretical biophysics of virus capsid assembly

Assembly and disassembly dynamics of viral capsids under varying physiological conditions and the fate of the enclosed genome.
